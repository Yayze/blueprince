# Rumpus Room
A uniquely modern fashioned gaming room, the Rumpus Room has provided many guests of the estate with countless hours of recreation. The room is outfitted with a record player, two television sets, a collection of board games and a mysterious antique fortune telling automaton named "Alzara".
