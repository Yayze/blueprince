### Rotating Gear

#### Switch
Multiple switches in the Rotating Gear room change the orientation or height of the gear.

![bp_reservoir_rotating-gear-switch.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_reservoir_rotating-gear-switch.png)

#### Mechanarium Blue Print
A permanent addition to the rotation of floorplans for the estate. #mechanarium 

![bp_reservoir_mechanarium-blueprint.png|800](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_reservoir_mechanarium-blueprint.png)

#### Tunnel
A large metal tunnel on the far side of the Rotating Gear. It eventually leads to the switch for the North door in the #antechamber, unlocking #room-46.
It also leads to a large sealed #red-door.
