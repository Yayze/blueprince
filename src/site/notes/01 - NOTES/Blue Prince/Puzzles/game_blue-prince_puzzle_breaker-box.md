### Keycard Entry System 
#solved

![bp_utility-room_breaker-box-keycard-system.png](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_utility-room_breaker-box-keycard-system.png)

A simple on-off switch. On by default. If turned off, disables all #security-door in the manor. By default, if this is turned off, security doors will be locked with no power unless overridden with the terminal in the #security-room.

If left "on", a #keycard-item will be required to open a #security-door.

### V.A.C. Indicators 
#solved

![bp_utility-room_breaker-box-vac.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_utility-room_breaker-box-vac.png)

- 6 indicator lights that can be toggled between 6 states (off - green - red - blue - purple - white). 
- By default, only off, green, red, and blue are available.
- An email from the #office terminal is required to solve this puzzle
- Part 1 - Set all lights to purple (to enable white)
	- Purple lights are triggered by clicking a blue light next to a red light. The red light will then turn purple.
	  
	  ![01 - NOTES/Blue Prince/Screenshots/bp_office_terminal-email-1.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_office_terminal-email-1.png)
	  
- Part 2 - Only one light can be set to white. Once part 1 is met, the next light clicked will turn white, the the rest of the combination from the #office terminal email 3 needs to be entered.
	  ![01 - NOTES/Blue Prince/Screenshots/bp_office_terminal-email-3.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_office_terminal-email-3.png)
	  
- Part 3 - Once the above sequence is entered correctly, a hidden wall behind the breaker box will open revealing a switch. When activated it will unlock the #gemstone-cavern on the grounds as well as a permanent unlock of starting with +2 gems everyday.

### Room Switches 
#unsolved 

![bp_utility-room_breaker-box-switches.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_utility-room_breaker-box-switches.png)

Removes or provides power to various rooms in the manor:
- #gymnasium - Unknown effect (**default: on**)
- #dark-room - Provides power/light to the Dark Room (**default: off**)
	- Leads to the #apple-orchard-puzzle solution.
- #garage - Provides power to the garage door switch (**default: off**)
	- Allows player to open the garage door and unlocking of the #west-gate 
	- Unlocks outer room - #shed
- #cabinet - Crossed out (**no switch**) - Unknown
