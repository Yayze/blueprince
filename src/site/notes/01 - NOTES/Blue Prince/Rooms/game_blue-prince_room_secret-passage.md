# Secret Passage
A small "deadend" room with an ordinary bookcase, that exemplifies that the best means to escape the confinements of our world has always been through books.

![bp_room_secret-passage.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_room_secret-passage.png)

> [!help] EFFECT
> Leads to a room of a color of your choice.

- [[#Message Behind Door]]

### Book Shelf
Pulling one of the books leads to drafting a room of that corresponding color.
