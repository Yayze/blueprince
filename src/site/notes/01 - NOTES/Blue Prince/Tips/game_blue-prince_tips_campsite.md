# Campsite
Due to the rules set for in your Great Uncle's will, you are not allowed to stay in the estate overnight.
Luckily, however, your scouting skills have been put to good use to fabricate a very respectable offsite sleeping quarters.

You may **CALL IT A DAY** at any time from the Blueprint Menu (**tab**) to return to the campsite for the night.
