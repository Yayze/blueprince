# Precipice
An elevator that leads down to the Precipice is revealed once the #four-flame-puzzle is solved.

### Elevator Switch
The switch for the elevator that leads to the Precipice. The 4 blue lights represent the 4 flames that need to be lit for the elevator to be functional. #four-flame-puzzle.

### Chess Board
A 5 x 9 grid, same as the estate, in the form of a chess board. Each row is numbered from 1 - 9, same as the ranks of the estate.
There are 5 blue chess pieces, one for each type of piece in a chess game (pawn, knight, bishop, rook, queen, king).

![bp_precipice_chess-board.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_precipice_chess-board.png)

---
### Breakable Wall
A breakable wall in the same room as the [[#Chess Board]]. It can be broken using a #power-hammer. 
Behind it is the word "**CASTLE**" spelled vertically with two blank spaces next to each letter.

![bp_precipice_castle.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_precipice_castle.png)
