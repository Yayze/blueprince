# Basement
Hidden behind a weak brick wall in the #sealed-entrance, it can be accessed by using a #power-hammer on the wall or via the #the-foundation by using the #basement-key found in the #antechamber.

### Locked Door
Locked door with a red gem in the center. Can be unlocked using the #basement-key found in the #antechamber. Leads to the #the-foundation elevator.

### Grandfather Clock
A clock with the small hand stopped at 9 and the large hand stopped between the 4 (20) and 5 (25).

### Wardrobe Drawing
A drawing, similar to what is found on the back of the safe door in the #boudoir, found on top one of the wardrobes in the Basement.

### Switch
A switch, when pressed (assuming the #boiler-room setting is correct), reveals a hidden passage behind a bookshelf to the #reservoir.
