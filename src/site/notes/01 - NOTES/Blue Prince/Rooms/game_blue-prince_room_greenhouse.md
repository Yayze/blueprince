# Greenhouse
This room is always warm as its glass walls and roof help to retain the sun's rays. The gardener should be pleased that the long-neglected flower beds have finally been blessed with the glistening gift from the sprinklers installed above.

### Broken Antechamber Lever 
#antechamber-lever-puzzle
In some green rooms there exists levers that can be pulled that open one of the doors of the Antechamber. The red on the icon indicates which door will be unlocked.

![bp_greenhouse_antechamber-south-door.png|300|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_greenhouse_antechamber-south-door.png)

The Greenhouse's lever is broken and requires the #lever-item to activate.
