# Secret Garden
A floorplan so secretive it had been removed from the drafting pool to avoid anyone unwanted accidentally stumbling upon it. This secluded garden, flanked by a variety of Mora Jai fruit trees, has served as a private retreat to at least two other generations of Sinclairs.

![bp_room_secret-garden.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_room_secret-garden.png)

> [!success] EFFECT
> *Spread* #fruit throughout the House.

Requires the #secret-garden-key to access.

### Crescent Moon Statue
A statue in the far center of the room inset in a wall. It acts as a secret door, unlocked via the #secret-garden-puzzle to reveal a lever for the #antechamber-lever-puzzle.

![bp_secret-garden_crescent-moon.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_secret-garden_crescent-moon.png)


---

### Secret Garden Puzzle
#secret-garden-puzzle 
A weather vane in the middle of the room contains three arrows, two of which are rotatable via a mechanism on either side of the garden.

![bp_secret-garden_weather-vane.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_secret-garden_weather-vane.png)

All 3 arrows must be turned to point toward the crescent moon statue to reveal a hidden lever for the #antechamber-lever-puzzle.
