# Abandoned Mine
The other side of the #reservoir that can be accessed via the basement door at the bottom of the #fountain with the #basement-key.

---
### Torch Chamber
A small round chamber, somewhat resembling the #rotunda, with 7 unlit candles and a center table with key-like symbols on it.

![bp_abandoned-mine_torch-chamber.png|800](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_abandoned-mine_torch-chamber.png)

---
### Center Table
The table in the center of the #torch-chamber with 5 rectangular looking shapes and 3 lines that seem to resemble keys pointing in different directions.

![bp_abandoned-mine_torch-chamber-center-table.png|800](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_abandoned-mine_torch-chamber-center-table.png)

### Ripped Underground Map
A ripped right-most piece to what appears to be the #underground-map found in the #sealed-entrance. However, partial cut out words cab be seen just above the legend spelling out "**_ gel points dreams**".
