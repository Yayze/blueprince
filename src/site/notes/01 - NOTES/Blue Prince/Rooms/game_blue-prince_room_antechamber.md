# Antechamber
From its root meaning "*The Room Before*", all signs and paths point toward the Antechamber. This mysterious sealed room -resting on the 9th Rank- may prove, however, quite an initial challenge to even reach, let alone enter. Still, you can't help but draw a connection between this chamber and #room-46 .

### Unlock
Can be unlocked via the #antechamber-lever-puzzle by unlocking one or more of the door by pulling levers found in the #greenroom, #secret-garden, and the #great-hall.

---
### Basement Key
Once the Antechamber is reached, a podium will rise from the floor with the #basement-key to unlock various other doors found in the #fountain, #basement / #the-foundation.

![bp_antechamber_basement-key.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_antechamber_basement-key.png)

---
### Note
Along with the #basement-key, a note reading "**To continue up, you must go down.**" is next to the key. Seems to indicate that in order to continue, we must go to the #basement.

![bp_antechamber_note.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_antechamber_note.png)

---
### Sealed Door
A sealed door with a #crescent-moon is shown blocking the north-facing door.

![bp_antechamber_sealed-door.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_antechamber_sealed-door.png)

---
### Room 46 Door
By pulling the lever found within the #inner-sanctum found in the #underground, the [[#Sealed Door]] will open and reveal the door to #room-46. Opening this door for the first time will show a cinematic and roll credits.

![bp_antechamber_room-46-door.png|300](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_antechamber_room-46-door.png)
