# Drawing Room
A social sitting room adorned wall to wall with artwork and drawings. An unfinished sketch of your uncle sits on an easel in the middle of the room, providing a vivid image of what were presumably his final days (depending on when the project was abandoned).

### Portrait of Sinclair
There is a portrait of Sinclar sitting on the chair in this very room. Over his right shoulder is a candle stick with the left most stick bent out. This is a clue to pull this secret lever that will reveal a locked safe behind a painting of a maiden.

### Drawing Room Safe
#drawing-room-safe #red-envelope-puzzle 
The safe, revealed behind a painting of a maiden by pulling the left candle-stick on the fireplace, contains 9-digits (0-9), a 'clear' and 'enter' button.
Inside is a red envelope marked with "**six**" in the bottom-left.

> [!note] SAFE CODE
> **0415**

![bp_drawing-room_safe.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_drawing-room_safe.png)

There are several paintings that remains static on the walls of this room that hint at the code for the safe. The number of maidens (04) and the number of slow guys (15) appears to be the code. The to these safes appears to always be the a date (MM-DD) of the red envelope letter inside!
