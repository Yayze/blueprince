# Fountain

- [[#Water Level]]
- [[#Fountain Door]]
---
### Water Level
The water in the Fountain can be lower or raised via the #pump-room.

### Fountain Door
Once the Fountain is completely drained, a locked door with a #red-gem can be accessed. A special key is needed to unlock the door.
