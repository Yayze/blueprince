# Watering Can
The first time you enter each #greenroom, use water to gain 1 #gem.
Limited Capacity: 3 water

![bp_item_watering-can.png|800](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_item_watering-can.png)

> [!note] REQUIREMENT
> The #pump-room must be sending water to the #greenhouse.
