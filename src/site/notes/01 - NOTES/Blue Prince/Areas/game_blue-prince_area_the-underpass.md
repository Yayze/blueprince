# The Underpass
A long metal-lined tunnel leading to a #red-door and the #north-switch in the #inner-sanctum that unlocks the north-facing door in the #antechamber leading to #room-46.

### The Red Door
A sealed #red-door connected to #the-underpass with a red high pressure condenser. A note for which can be found in the #sealed-entrance. Needs to be activated from the #boiler-room by providing power to the red condenser.

![bp_the-underpass_the-red-door.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_the-underpass_the-red-door.png)

#### Outer Rotating Gear
Behind the red door is a large cavernous area where The Underpass runs through connected to the #rotating-gear. Here, steps can be found leading up to the second level of the Rotating Gear room.

![bp_underpass_outer-rotating-gear.png|800](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_underpass_outer-rotating-gear.png)

##### Locked Gate
A locked gate via a 4-letter combination lock, with one letter - "8". The combination, as per the #duo-picture-puzzle, is a date (**MAY8**). Behind the gate is [[#Red Envelope 1]], the [[#Treasure Trove Blueprint]], a #gem, and [[#Foreman's Log 102]].

![bp_underpas_locked-gate-lock.png|500](/img/user/01%20-%20NOTES/Blue%20Prince/Screenshots/bp_underpas_locked-gate-lock.png)
